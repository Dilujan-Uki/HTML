BlueJscript is not

A learning enviornment:Do not expect BlueJscrpit to teach you the syntax of Javascript.To find out
about Javascript you will need to look at the refrences in the main BlueJscirpt site.

A complete browser system: Most of the BlueJscirpt exaples have been written in standard HTML.
But they contains links to other site that use the most advanced HTML and they are not compatible
with the standard viewer that is embedded here. If you want to browse the internet use your preferred browser.

A tutorial on Webservices:You will realize that you can drive BlueJscirpt through Webservices. There
will be examples on how to do it but you will need to do your homework on understanding the technology.
